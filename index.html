    About Us

    Louise Bistro Est. 1910

    Louise Bistro open its doors for business in 1889.What was initially a sandwich shop and lottery ticket outlet in 1939 blossomed into a thriving bar and later a respected family restaurant in Charlotte. Founded by Louise Parker, Louise Bistro soon became the meeting place for music and entertainment, civil rights, and culture in The Carolinas.Today Louise Bistro remains family owned and operated. and remains the premier restaurant for authentic soul food Cuisine.


    The Parker Family enjoys serving its regular customers, tourists, and locals. They also remain a stopping place for politicians, musicians, visual artists, and famous athletes. Louise Bistro has had the pleasure of serving both President George W. Bush and President Barack Obama, Jackie Robinson, Bill Russell, Quincy Jones and a list of others.


    For over seventy years the Parker Family worked tenaciously to uplift their family and community as best they could through their faith, successful management of their Restaurant, active involvement in social justice activities that fostered civil liberties, and full inclusion of all, and support of the creative and culinary arts..
